Accommodations

Lander Hall guests: The location is 1201 NE Campus Pkwy in the University District, a short walk from the conference. Rooms have towels, linens, and free wireless. Lander Hall has dining facilities. Here is more about Lander hall accommodations.

Transportation instructions are here. If you are driving, find a metered parking space near the Conference check-in Desk. You can purchase parking permits valid for the entire stay in nearby lots during check-in. The daily rate for parking in UW lots is $15 Monday through Friday and $5 on Saturday from 7 am to noon. There is no charge to park after noon on Saturday all day on Sunday. Disability parking permits or other special parking accommodations should be arranged in advance through Conference Services
