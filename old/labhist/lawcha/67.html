Industrial Nostalgia and Heritage Preservation

Stefan Berger1, Thomas Welskopp2, Marion Fontaine3, Ruben Vega4, Erik Eklund5, Jana Golombek1
1Ruhr University Bochum, 2University of Bielefeld, 3University of Lille, 4University of Oviedo, 5Federation University, Australia

Abstract

The proposed panel will explore the contested terrain of industrial heritage and labour history. We intend to focus on how class is present or absent in industrial heritage and what this says about the relational position of class to other master narratives such as race, gender, ethnicity, colonization or re-occupation.
