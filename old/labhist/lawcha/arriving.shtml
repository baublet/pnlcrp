Transportation to Residence Hall or UW Campus

Lander Hall, the residence hall where many are staying (1201 NE Campus Pkwy), is next door to Alder Commons where we are holding the Thursday night welcome reception and plenary and a few blocks from the UW campus and Mary Gates Hall where sessions will meet Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

From the Airport to Lander Hall and University District Hotels

Taxi and Shuttle Express Service: Taxi (25 minutes) and Shuttle Express are located on the 3rd floor parking garage of the Sea-Tac International Airport. Taxi will cost about $50 and Shuttle Express $24.75. Tips are expected on both.

Light Rail/Bus Service: Light rail (called Link) is direct from airport to UW campus but the UW station is a Â¾ mile walk from Lander Hall where many are staying. It is much closer to Mary Gates Hall where most conference sessions will be held. To get to Lander from Sea-Tac International Airport, the trip involves both light rail and buses (separate tickets) and will take at least one hour. Follow airport signs to Link light rail system heading north to Seattle. Pay the fare before boarding Link light rail. Exit at any of the downtown Seattle transit centers (International District/Chinatown, Pioneer Square, University Street and Westlake). Purchase bus ticket and board a northbound bus #71, 72

Driving to Campus, University District, Lander Hall

Via Interstate 5
Take exit 169, NE 45th Street and turn east (right if traveling north, left if traveling south) onto 45th Street. Travel east on NE 45th Street to Roosevelt Way NE. Turn south (right) on Roosevelt Way NE. Continue driving south until you pass NE 42nd Street. Merge into the left turn lane and turn left onto NE Campus Parkway at the last stop light prior to the University Bridge. Lander Hall will be on your right. The Conference Desk is located in Lander Hall. (1201 NE Campus Pkwy),

Via WA State Route 520
Take the Montlake Boulevard NE exit. Turn north onto Montlake Boulevard NE; quickly merge into the far left lane and proceed over the Montlake Bridge. At the first traffic light after the bridge, turn left (west) onto NE Pacific Street; continue past the UW Medical Center on your left. Turn right (north) onto Brooklyn Avenue NE. After you pass NE 40th Street, Lander Hall will be on your left. The Conference Desk is located in Lander Hall. (1201 NE Campus Pkwy).

Parking:

The daily rate for parking in UW lots and garages is $15 Monday through Friday and $5 on Saturday from 7 am to noon. There is no charge to park after noon on Saturday all day on Sunday.

Guests staying in Lander Hall should find a metered parking space near the Conference Check-in Desk. You can purchase parking permits valid for the entire stay in nearby lots during check-in. Disability parking permits or other special parking accommodations should be arranged in advance through Conference Services. [more]

If you are driving to campus for the conference events in Mary Gates Hall, it is easiest to park in the Central Plaza Garage with entrance at the intersection of 15th Ave NE and NE 41 St. Same rates apply.

Transit to and from Downtown

Bus service from the University District (near Lander Hall) is 20-25 minutes to downtown. Catch the 70 at Campus Parkway and 12th. across from Lander Hall. Buses run every 10-15 minutes. Get off at 3rd and Pine near Pike Place Market and downtown locations. Return on the 70 (3rd and Pike) or 49 (4th and Pike).

To and from Mary Gates Hall and other central campus locations, it is faster to take Link lightrail from the UW station. This involves a short walk down the Rainier Vista from MGH.

King County metro trip planner provides schedules for these routes and can help you reach other locations.
