Working Towards a Global History of Social Movements

Stefan Berger1, Michael McCann2, Marc Steinberg3, Holger Nehring4, Geoff Eley5, Leon Fink6
1Ruhr University Bochum, 2University of Washington, 3Smith College, 4University of Stirling, 5University of Michigan, 6University of Illinois-Chicago

Abstract

This roundtable will discuss the publication of 'The Palgrave Studies in the History of Social Movements' flagship volume 'History of Social Movements - Global Perspectives' which is scheduled to publish in the spring of 2017. The editors, Stefan Berger and Holger Nehring, will present key findings of the volume and the respondents, Michael McCann, Marc Steinberg and Geoff Eley will each present a critique of the volume in question.
