N.A.F.T.A. (Not A Fair Trade for All)

Fred Lonidier
UCSD Visual Arts Dept. & AFT

Abstract

From the many works I have done as part of my "N.A.F.T.A..." series, I will select a few to project with commentary about them including quotations of workers, activists with an emphasis on cross-border solidarity. Much will depend on the time I am allotted should my submission be accepted.
