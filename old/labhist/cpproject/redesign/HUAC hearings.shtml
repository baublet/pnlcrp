U.S. Congress House UnAmerican Activities Committee Hearings in Seattle, 1954, 1955, 1956

U.S. Congress House Unamerican Activities Committee travelled to Seattle for three sets of hearings in the mid 1950s, demanding testimony from suspected Communists and threatening to imprison those who refused to answer questions about their own political activities and the lives of others.
