My People Are Rising: Memoir of a Black Panther Party Captain, by Aaron Dixon

Co-founder and captain of the Seattle chapter, Aaron Dixon's memoir is a richly personal account published in 2012.

 It's About Time: Black Panther Party Legacy and Alumni

It's About Time is the official online source of information about the national activities of the Black Panther Party. It includes a substantial section on the Seattle Chapter with some photographs and pamphlets.

Mapping American Social Movements Project

The Mapping American Social Movements Project provides interactive maps of Black Panther Party offices and events in Seattle and five other cities.
