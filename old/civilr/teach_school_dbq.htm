For Teachers

School Segregation in Seattle Lesson Plan
(Document Based Question)
Targeted Age: 11th Grade
Targeted Time Period: 1 90-minute Block

created by Teresa Frizell
Objective: Students will synthesize their prior knowledge of southern-based Civil Rights movement with new knowledge about Seattle’s own struggle through their response to the Document Based Question: “Compare and/or contrast school segregation in the southern United States with school segregation in Seattle.”
