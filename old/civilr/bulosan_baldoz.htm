Carlos Bulosan Special Section

 The FBI Files of Carlos Bulosan

by Rick Baldoz

The FBI files on Carlos Bulosan from the 1940s and 1950s shed light on more than just the life of a renowned writer and labor activist.  They also illuminate two interrelated issues: the trajectory of Filipino American political consciousness in the era of anti-colonial upheavals sweeping across the globe, and the ideological machinations of U.S. authorities working to contain the spread of domestic and international radicalism during the Cold War.
