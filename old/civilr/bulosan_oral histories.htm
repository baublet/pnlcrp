 Oral Histories:
Josephine Patrick, Chris Mensalvas

Here are oral histories with Chris Mensalvas and Josephine Patrick recounting their special friendships with Carlos Bulosan and filling in details about his life and activism.  

Josephine Patrick interview

Bulosan's campanion in the last six years of his life, Josephine Patrick shares memories of the writer and discusses her own lifetime of activism in a 1998 interview with Cindy Domingo.

Chris Mensalvas interview

Mensalvas had known Bulosan since the early 1930s. They had worked in the fields together and organized unions together. After Mensalvaz became president of Local 7, the Cannery workers union, in 1949, he hired Bulosan to edit the union's yearbook.

In 1974, Apolonio Buyagawan interviewed Mensalvas about his own life and his long friendship with Bulosan.

This audio recording is made available by the Labor Archives and Special Collections, University of Washington Libraries.
